How it works

This tool helps you find the optimal price for your product by:

  1. Collecting real market data for your product from N11.com
  2. Analyzing the relationship between price and consumer interest
  3. Estimating demand at different price points
  4. Calculating the price that maximizes your profit

Note: The calculation depends on the quality of available market data and your input parameters.
